Word-Tracker

Word-Tracker is a fun Discord bot that I worked on in collaboration with my friend. It is a project that I maintain and work on from time to time. We started work on it almost 5 years ago and it is still working!

The purpose of this bot is to keep a list of words that it should keep track of. When one of those words is sent as a message in a Discord server, the bot will look that user up in a database and tick up a counter.

The bot keeps track of how many tracked words have been said. It does not keep track of individual words
